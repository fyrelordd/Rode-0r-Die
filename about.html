About This Site

What Is This?

Westeros: Ride or Die is an interactive experience that assigns you a character from the world of game of Thrones... someone who might stand beside you when the realm burns or walk away when you need them the most.

From loyal wolves to cunning lions, this project explores the grey morality that makes Westeros iconic. Your match might stand with you to the end or disappear when winter truly comes.

“In the game of thrones, loyalty is a luxury few can afford.”

How It Works

On the decision page, entering your name is essential. After which you are required to choose your fate either by House, Personality, or by a Random Selection. Six houses have been featured in the "House" section with 3 - 5 characters from each House.

The Personality section comprises of nine different Personalities or Traits with the featured characters ranging from 3 - 5 for each, while the Random Selection consists of 20 featured characters.

The House section is strictly made up of the characters from the mentioned house, the Personality section comprises of both the characters from "House" section and a few other noble houses across Westeros.

The Random section, however comprises of a mix of characters from the noble houses and a handful of a others like sellswords, hedge knights etc ranging from the Seven Kingdoms to Essos and beyond.

The featured "Traits or Personality" are : Ambitious, Brave, Cruel, Cunning, Honorable, Loyal, Ruthless, Strategic, and Wise.
